## Purpose

This milestone refines your project by providing more detailed information on your methodology and anticipated results. It builds on your initial proposal (M01) by elaborating on how you will achieve your prediction goal.

## Components

### 1. Revised Prediction Goal and Objectives
Refine your prediction goal if necessary and clearly state your project objectives.

### 2. Data Preparation Process
- **Data Cleaning and Preprocessing:** the steps you will take to clean and prepare the data.
- **Feature Engineering:** any new features you plan to create to improve model performance.

### 3. Methodology
- **Predictive Analytics Methods:** a detailed outline of the analytical methods, algorithms, or models you will employ.
- **Evaluation Metrics:** how you will measure performance (e.g., accuracy, F1-score, RMSE).

### 4. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Present initial findings where possible — summary statistics and visualizations (charts, histograms, scatter plots) that reveal patterns or trends.

### 5. Anticipated Challenges
Identify potential challenges (e.g., data-quality issues, overfitting) and propose strategies to mitigate them.

## Submission Expectations

- **Length:** 2–3 pages.
- Include visualizations and graphs where appropriate.
- Well-organized with subheadings and bullet points; builds on M01 by incorporating feedback and further refinement.
